# meghana-Data-Analyst-internship-portfolio
Overview

This portfolio showcases the successful completion of an end-to-end Data Analyst Internship project focused on Customer Segmentation &amp; Marketing Analytics.

The project covers the complete analytics lifecycle, from data cleaning and preprocessing to exploratory data analysis, interactive dashboard development, data storytelling, and statistical validation. The objective was to transform raw customer data into actionable business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

Internship Roadmap

Task 1 – Data Immersion & Wrangling
Cleaned and preprocessed raw customer data
Handled missing values and duplicate records
Standardized categorical variables
Performed feature engineering to create meaningful business attributes

Task 2 – Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) & Business Intelligence
Analyzed customer demographics and spending behavior
Explored product preferences and purchasing trends
Evaluated campaign performance and customer engagement
Generated business insights through data visualization

Task 3 – Deep-Dive Analysis & Interactive Dashboarding
Built an interactive Tableau dashboard
Developed KPI cards and business visualizations
Analyzed customer segments, purchase channels, and complaint trends
Designed a dashboard for stakeholder decision-making

Task 4 – Data Storytelling & Statistical Validation
Created a business-focused presentation of analytical findings
Performed hypothesis testing using an Independent T-Test
Validated the relationship between customer income and spending behavior
Developed data-driven business recommendations
